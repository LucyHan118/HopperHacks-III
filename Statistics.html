Plastic Consumption

There is over 380 million tonnes of plastic generated each year worldwide.

The US uses the most plastic out of all the countries in the world.


Plastic waste is only increasing and by around 9% each year.

Around 165 million tonnes of plastic ends up in the ocean, killing and endangering many species.

Less than 9% of plastic ends up being recycled, and most are sent off to be dumped in landfills.

Over 30% of our plastic waste ends up in the environment untouched.
